Play Board Help

On this screen you solve your puzzle! You can "Long Touch" the playboard (touch a single point for a second or so) to bring up the Zoom menu. Pressing the Menu button provides the following options:

  • Show Errors
    Highlights unsolved or incorrect boxes in red.
  • Reveal
    Presents the option to reveal the correct solution to a box, word or the whole puzzle. Once revealed "cheated" boxes have a pink background.
  • Clues
    Takes you to a screen where you can browse the across and down clues for the whole puzzle. You can also reach this screen by tapping the clue line at the top of the screen.
  • Info
    Shows information about the puzzle you are playing.
  • Help
    Displays this screen
  • Settings
    Takes you to the application settings screen.
