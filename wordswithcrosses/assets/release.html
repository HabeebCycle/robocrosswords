Words With Crosses v1.0.0

Welcome to Words With Crosses! The wonderful Shortyz Crosswords app has not seen any updates in quite some time, so with a heavy heart I decided to fork the project into what you see before you. That that was possible at all is thanks to Shortyz' being open-source.

Many thanks to Robert Cooper for creating Shortyz.

For the previous release notes of Shortyz, see this page. You may also make a donation to Robert Cooper there if you so choose.

Words With Crosses is free and will always remain free. It is a side project of mine done in my spare time. If you'd like to make a donation to help further the development, please email me at <wordswithcrosses@adamrosenfield.com>, and I'll set up a PayPal donation link.
